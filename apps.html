Volunteer Jobs

Applications

All jobs are volunteer positions. Below are our open positions. Please read through the descriptions carefully before applying. Nothing can be edited! You will get a response as soon as possible. If you don't receive an answer within 2 weeks you may consider it as declined. Any questions regarding the application form can be asked on email [email protected].

Moderator

We are looking for active moderators to help us out with moderating on our platforms. If you are interested in doing this you have come to the right place!


If your application is accepted you will be contacted on Discord for an interview. This means you MUST have a working microphone to join our staff team.


What you will be doing

  • Moderating the BatMod Discord server to enforce the rules

  • Helping people who experience problems with BatMod (the game)

  • Answering questions relevant to BatMod

  • Possibly more in the feature as we keep expanding our platforms


Requirements

  • 16 years of age or older

  • Owning a legal copy of Minecraft

  • Read and speak fluent English

  • Being helpful and have good manners

  • Being an active member of our community

  • Above intermediate understanding of a commonly used operating system

  • Being open minded to new ideas


Bonus Points

  • Knowing a second language

  • Experience with moderating/administration

  • Experience with Minecraft crashes/errors

  • Programming knowledge

Proofreader

We are looking for active proofreaders to help us out with checking suggested translations on our translation website. If you are interested in doing this you have come to the right place!


If your application is accepted you will be contacted on Discord for an interview. This does not require a working microphone. However, it would be much appreciated if you are able to respond in the voice call, but this is not a requirement and you can optionally reply in chat to our questions.


What you will be doing

  • Quality controlling translations in your language

  • Giving feedback to other translators

  • Translating your language

  • Most likely be working in teams


Requirements

  • Read and write fluently in your language

  • Read English at an intermediate level


Bonus Points

  • Experience with any translation tool such as POEditor, Crowdin, Weblate

  • Experience with working in teams
